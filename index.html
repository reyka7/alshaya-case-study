01 · Context

Malls aren't just retail spaces.

In Kuwait, and across the Gulf, malls are largely a social space.

The Global Retail Concept

Many franchise stores are designed around a western and linear shopper journey.

The Gulf Mall Concept

In Kuwait and across the Gulf, a mall visit can be a family outing, a walking route, a food stop, a social catch-up, a children’s detour, and a shopping trip — all in one journey.

02 · Problem

The store is designed. The journey around it is not.

A day at the mall often includes other side-quests and activities. These moments influence dwell time, comfort, purchase decisions, and movement between brands.

03 · Opportunity

05 · Service concept

The Local Fit

A set of experience interventions that help Alshaya design beyond the store — across waiting, pausing, and walking.

01

Companion Comfort

Companions shape how long shoppers stay.

Make waiting feel intentional through comfortable seating, living-room-style fitting areas, charging points, small hospitality cues, and shared decision moments near high-dwell stores.

Core idea When companions feel comfortable, shoppers get more time to browse, try, and decide.
Best fit Fashion, Beauty, Baby care
02

Pause & Resume

The pause is not the problem. Losing the customer in the journey is.

Alshaya can help shoppers continue later through item holds, QR save points, saved sizes, Aura reminders, and staff prompts that make returning feel easy.

Core idea Let customers step away without losing purchase intent.
Best fit Fitting rooms, Beauty counters, High-consideration retail
03

Aura Walk Loops

Turn everyday mall walking into a healthier, more rewarding journey.

Aura Walk builds on the existing habit of walking inside malls. As customers complete distance or step milestones, they earn Aura points that can be redeemed at their favourite Alshaya brands.

This also aligns with Kuwait’s Healthy Cities and Health Malls direction, where malls are being explored as everyday spaces that can encourage healthier behaviours.

Core idea Connect wellness, movement, and retail engagement through Aura.
